About Evergreen International School

Dedicated to providing exceptional early childhood education with love, care, and global perspective

Our Mission

At Evergreen International School, we believe every child is unique and deserves a nurturing environment to grow, learn, and explore. Our mission is to provide a safe, loving, and stimulating atmosphere where children can develop their full potential while fostering global awareness and cultural understanding.

Our Values

  • Love and compassion in every interaction
  • Safety and security as our top priority
  • Quality education through play-based learning
  • Strong partnerships with families
  • Global citizenship and cultural diversity
  • Environmental responsibility and sustainability

Our Programs & Curriculum

Age-appropriate programs designed to foster growth, creativity, and global awareness

Little Sprouts

Our youngest learners enjoy sensory play, music, and gentle activities that promote motor skills and social development in a secure, multicultural environment.

6 months - 18 months

Global Explorers

Toddlers discover the world through hands-on activities, art, music, and early language development with introduction to multiple languages and cultures.

18 months - 3 years

Pre-K International

School readiness program focusing on early literacy, numeracy, social skills, and global citizenship to prepare for elementary success.

3 - 5 years

Important Information

School Hours: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Class Size: Maximum 12 students per class
Teacher Ratio: 1:4 for infants, 1:6 for toddlers
